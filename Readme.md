--------------------------------------------------Modpack Description-------------------------------------------------

DISCLAIMER: What follows is the Pitch for the Modpack, here meaning the merged output acquired through TKMM. To understand what the Patch does please refer to the Section titled "IMPORTANT NOTE" above.

Explore an expanded and overhauled version of Hyrule far grander than the one seen in Tears of the Kingdom. With constituent mods such as Challenge Mode, Depths of the Kingdom, The Second Quest, Sky Islands Expansion and much more you'll be sure to find a great plethora of new content to discover.

This is a handcrafted, expert Modpack and as such the difficulty curve is one of its many targets for overhaul, meaning the game starts comparatively vanilla regarding difficulty in the beginning, but shall even challenge Veterans of the Wilds the more you reach the outerlands of the Abyss. There are even twelve Superbosses that surpass all but the Primordial Demon Reborn.

Challenge Mode made by Echocolat et. al. all the credits belong to them. It gives us our beloved Lynel weapons back, adds Golden Enemies to the game and offers to us the opportunity to challenge the twelve Superboss aforementioned.

Depths of the Kingdom made by PotatoeFried17 et. al. all the credits belong to them. It turns the Depths (in EaO termed "Abyss") from a World of Darkness into a strange and eerily otherworldly place filled to the brim with nightmarish creatures and valuable loot.
In Expanded and Overhauled this overhauled version of the Depths serves the role of the games Challenge Zone!
You may enter early, but as long as you haven't mastered the Lands above your chances of survival are negligible at best! This place is the home to Titans outweighing and outpunching even the Demons of Gold. 
Be wise and come prepared!

The Second Quest made by Deadpanned and YourDiamondDog et. al. all credits belong to them. It serves as the foundation and inspiration for the overhaul you'll see with Expanded and Overhauled, as this mod alone already overhauls a majority of enemy-, material- and gears-values such as health, heal, damage and defense.
It also grants to us the Mighty Zonai Devices who have withstood the tides of time for a hundred millenia.
Not only that! It also introduces tiers to enemies that had none in the vanilla game such as Aerocuda and the Minifroxes.

Weapons of Legends Redux made by Real Princess Zelda, Alasazar and Tach9, all credits belong to them. It adds a ton of legendary weapons to the game with all of them being from older Zelda titles. They are all spread throughout the Kingdom of Hyrule and in the Lands above, beneath and upperyard. Some will be guarded, some have to be unlocked, some await to be found, but all have the duty to guide you on the path to the Ultimate Battle!

Sky Islands Expansion made by Der Zelda Player et. al. all credits belong to them. It fixes a fundamental issue of the vanilla game, namely the fact that there is only ~5km2 sky islands on otherwise ~130km2 total map of playable areas. This Mod aims to increase the amount of upperyard, filling the new heavenly regions to the fullest with new content now coming to life.
Go forth and discover new lands no mortal eyes have afore seen!

Depths of History made by Cargocult, all credits belong to them. It let's us explore the true extent of what has led to this exact point in time! Discover what has taken place in the Lands beneath and how it all connects back to what takes place in the Lands above. The best about all of this? The tales told here are lore accurate. To get started you'll revisit the Keeper of the Slate once you fallen into the belly of the World once. Are you ready?

Ganon's Cursed Blade made by -alpha-, all credits belong to them. It brings the Wraith Kings directly to you, here in Expanded and Overhauled. In EaO they serve as markers of progress on your journey across the Lands. If you are capable of slaying them all then you shall be ready to tackle the evils hiding in the shadow of the World!

Revamped Master Quest Main Dungeons made by DarkHylianSlayer, all credits belong to them. It brings to you every Dungeon overhauled in the Image of Ocarina of Time's Master Quest Dungeons. It will throw at you new and unexpected puzzles and a increased variety of enemies to faze off against. If you come unprepared then you'll be stuck! 
Do you dare to enter the Domains of the Archdemons!

-----------------------------------------------Content yet included--------------------------------------------------

Sooga's Revenge made by GunBot800 et. al. all credits belong to the them. It brings an extended Yiga storyline past the final battle against Kohga. This is achieved by introducing Sooga and his own loyal companion, Yiga Generals and much more you shall discover on your quest to find the truth behind their worshipp of the Primordial Demon Reborn.
Will you be able to stop the Yiga's reign of Terror once and for all?!

Trials for the Chosen Hero made by Aster et. al. all credits belong to them. It brings back the most-awaited quest over to Tears of the Kingdom from Breath of the Wild, namely the Trials of the Sword alongside many other things including Story expansions. 
Are up to the task, dear Hero?

---------------------------------------------Exclusive Content and Changes--------------------------------------------

Expanded and Overhauled is not only just a multicomp between all the mods abovementioned, but a Mod that aims to offer a unified and unique experience not found anywhere else. This includes new quests, more lore, every handbook, pouch entry, character profile and quest log changed from third-person to first-person narration giving you a glimpse into how Link thinks and feels about the world which surrounds him and all that inhabits it.

Where neccessary NPC dialogue can and will either partly or completely be rewritten. This serves the purpose of bridging the gap between Botw and Totk, making the latter a proper narrative sequel to the former.
Such changes include making Symin stop being a liar by omission and actually tell his Students that it was Link who has slain the Calamity, which actually everyone kinda ought to know about. Another change is to make the relationship between Link and Zelda much better understandable in the sense that they are meant to represent the trope of 'light in the darkness' and 'the seed of the uprising from the rubble'.
